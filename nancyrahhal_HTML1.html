Codi


What is Codi?

Codi is a leading non-governmental organisation (NGO) in Lebanon, operating in 2017, that provides accessible and transformative education driving economic growth and social change. Open and accessible to all, our Core programme combines a coding boot camp and a leadership school, and it is designed and continuously improved to cultivate lifelong learners and Full-Stack Web Developers (89% employment rate).


What programmes does Codi offer in Lebanon?

Currently, Codi offers its Core (Full-Stack Web Development Programme) in Beirut and Tripoli. They also run community-based workshops yearly (check their social media for more information).


Do you qualify for the Core?

To apply for the core Full-Stack Web Development programme, you must:

  • Be 17 years old minimum
  • Be highly motivated to change your life and find employment in the tech sector
  • Posses basic English language skills
